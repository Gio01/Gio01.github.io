About me

Currently, an InfoSec student studying at Purdue University. Before my current enrollment, I was studying Computer Science at DePauw University where I took interest in Web Development. Specifically, I did some Full Stack Web Development with Javascript. During my last year at DePauw, I published my thesis "Cyber Security in the Healthcare Industry" where I researched the many vectors of attack that exist within hospital settings. This included topics such as Medical Devices, Social Engineering, and Networking. From the research I had done on this thesis I decided that I wanted to learn more about security and hence I decided to pursue my current Master's in Information Security to learn more about security. From my experience, there is a gap between the development of software for functionality and the development of software with security in mind. I want to bridge this gap to help make software both secure and functional for users to use safely.
